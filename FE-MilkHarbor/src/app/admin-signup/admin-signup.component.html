Admin Signup

The Admin Registration page serves as the initial step for administrators to access the MilkHarbor Milk Collection System.
Administrators play a crucial role in managing farmers, overseeing milk collection, and maintaining system integrity. To register as an admin, fill in the required information accurately.

Password Instruction

  1. Ensure your password is a minimum of 8 characters.
  2. Include at least one special character (e.g., "@", #, $).
  3. Include at least one numeric digit (0-9).
  4. Use a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters.
